General Medicine

General medicine or internal medicine, is a branch of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of adult diseases. It is a primary care specialty, which means that they are often the first point of contact for our patients.

Cardiology

Cardiology is a branch of medicine that specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases of the heart and blood vessels. Cardiology has made great progress in recent years and it keeps progressing.

Neurology

Neurology is a branch of medicine that specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of disorders of the nervous system. This includes the brain, spinal cord, nerves... Research in neurology is an active and rapidly growing field.

Radiology

Radiology is a medical specialty that uses imaging techniques such as x-rays, computed tomography (CT), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), ultrasound, and nuclear medicine to diagnose and treat disease.
